Marathon and a peek into Florence

 December 2, 2014   Florence    running travel

Yesterday was the 31st City marathon of Florence. It’s a great experience. Lots of runners (12,000+) and spectators (they cheered us). The city has several big cathedral, museum, and other historical structures. After all, the city of da Vinci. The marathon route covers the major touristic points. There is a river through the city, and we ran alongside it several times. You get a lot of memories to take with you after running 42.2 KM in such a wonderful place. Grazie Firenze!

And today morning I climbed the Bell tower (414 steps) and the top of the Duomo. You get some really magnificent view of Florence from the top.
