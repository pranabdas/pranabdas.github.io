Hajiali at Dusk

 May 20, 2012  Mumbai  journal

Today evening I went to Hajiali by cycling with camera. I expected some nice sunset photo with Hajiali in the frame. But today there was not much clouds, so not much climax. Though I did capture some nice photograph, I was expecting something better.

Then the sun went down. I decided to head back. I crossed the road, and looked back from the other side of the lane. Aww… this is what I saw.
