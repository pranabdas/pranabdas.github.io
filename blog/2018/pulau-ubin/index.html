Pulau Ubin

 May 6, 2018  Singapore  cycling

Pulau Ubin is northeast of mainland Singapore. We took a morning ferry from Changi Ferry Terminal. Got to the island and rented bikes to move around the island. There are restaurants, no need to worry about survival. The island is big enough to spend a whole day. Tropical forest, mangroves, coconut trees, some sand beaches, small lakes. There are some watchtowers for panoramic views. Depending on the timing, you might be able to see some beautiful coral reefs. If it is high-tide, they might be underwater. Birds, monkeys, crabs, lizards and mosquitoes all around. Don’t forget to bring insect repellant and wear full sleeve. It also lines up with one of Changi landing runway, you will see airplanes descending very low every couple of minutes.
