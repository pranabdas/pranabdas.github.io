Waiheke Island

 August 10, 2019  New Zealand  travel

Waiheke is about one hour by boat from Auckland Central. The ferry arrives at Matiatia Wharf. Then intra-island busses can be used to move around or just walk. You have to plan which points you are going to cover; seeing each and every corner is not feasible in a single day. There are several beaches, beautiful Oneroa is one of them.
