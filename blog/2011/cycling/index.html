A day of cycling

April 17, 2011   Mumbai 

Sand dunes at Nagaon beach, Maharashtra, India.

Today we (Deep, Ravitej, and myself) cycled about 90 KM. It was a hot summer day, but we enjoyed it. First cycled to Gate way of India, took a boat to Mandawa with cycles onboard. We watched the beautiful sunrise from the boat. Then cycling to Alibag, to Nagaon, to Revdanda. All these are beaches, and connected by roads along the beautiful coastline. This sand pattern we saw at the Nagaon beach.
