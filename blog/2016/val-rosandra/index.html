A trail walk at Val Rosandra

March 19, 2016   Trieste 

We had an enchanting trail walk at Val Rosandra. It was a sunny day. There were nice panoramic views, with mountains in our left and right, and in front and in back too. We followed along the river, and occasionally crossing back and forth as water level was low in this period of year. The water stream made some wonderful canyons over the years. Then there was a tall beautiful waterfall. At the end we had some refreshments at a home-like restaurant. We had drinks, salami with cheese, olives, and warm breads. It was a wonderful day deep in the nature with friends.
