A trail walk at Val Rosandra

March 20, 2016   Trieste

We had an enchanting trail walk at Val Rosandra. It was a sunny day. There were nice panoramic views, with mountains in our left and right, and in front and in back too. We walked along the river Val Rosandra, and occasionally crossing back and forth. The water stream made some wonderful cavities over the years. Then there was a tall beautiful waterfall. At the end we had some refreshments at a home-like restaurant. We had drinks, salami with cheese, olives, and warm breads. It was a wonderful day deep in the nature and with good friends :)
